# Security Procedures & Checklists

This section provides quick-reference procedures for pre-service checks, emergency responses, and general expectations.

---

## Pre-Service Checklist
- Unlock main doors (as scheduled)
- Conduct a walkthrough of all hallways and bathrooms
- Check children’s areas for hazards or unsecured doors
- Verify radios are powered and communicating
- Position team members at entrances and hallways
- Note any suspicious activity or unattended items

---

## During Service
- Maintain observation of entrances and high-traffic hallways
- Support ushers and staff as needed
- Conduct rotating patrols every 10–15 minutes
- Monitor parking lot if coverage allows
- Maintain communication via radio

---

## Post-Service
- Assist with crowd flow and parking as needed
- Check children’s areas and classrooms again
- Lock designated doors per security policy
- Ensure no one is lingering in restricted areas
- Prepare incident notes if something occurred

---

## Emergency Response
### Medical Emergency
1. Call 911 immediately.
2. Notify team via radio.
3. Send a team member to guide EMS to patient.
4. Clear space for responders.

### Fire / Evacuation
1. Pull alarm if fire is visible.
2. Assist staff with evacuation routes.
3. Check rooms only if safe.
4. Direct all individuals to assembly points.

### Suspicious Individual
1. Observe discreetly and notify team.
2. Maintain safe distance.
3. Alert staff or pastor if escalation is needed.
4. Call police if threat is imminent.

### Active Threat
1. Call 911 immediately.
2. Direct congregants to run, hide, or barricade.
3. Do not attempt confrontation unless trained.
4. Provide information to law enforcement on arrival.

---

## Radio Communication Guidelines
- Keep messages short and clear.
- Use plain language (no codes).
- Report incidents immediately.
- Confirm receipt of important messages.
